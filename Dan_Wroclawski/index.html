Tech Co.'s and Startups to Watch

There has never been a more exciting time to be in the technology world. Companies big and small have been creating unique hardware and software with the opportunity to change the world. Google has created self-driving cars and glasses-based computers. Samsung and Sony have created smartwatches, and Apple is rumored to be on their heels. And small startups have been making big waves too. Snapchat is changing the way people share moments and connect. Foursquare has been making it easier to find new places to go. The changes are so big it's hard to keep track. Here are some of the players to follow.

Tech Giants

The following companies are internationally recognized and a force in the industry. They all have the ability to make or break startups and influence the fate of humanity lives of consumers.

Startups

The following are some young and nimble startups that you should expect to hear more about in the future:

Foursquare
A location discovery service and social network available online and via mobile apps.
FrontBack
A photo app that stitches together photos taken with a smartphone's front and rear-facing cameras.
Inside
A news app and website that aggregates stories into bite-size articles and links to longer form articles from competitors.
Snapchat
A private photo messaging application that utilizes self-destructing photos to maintain a sense of security.
Tinder
A dating app that uses photos and quick swiping to match people with potential dates.
