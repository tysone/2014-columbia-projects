Ten Cities, Ten Concerts to Hit on Your Eurotrip

Paris, France

AWOLNATION is an American electronic rock band. All of the songs on their album Megalithic Symphony are as epic as their top-ten single Sail, which is apparently the theme song for an alien abduction, so pretty epic. They're on tour, follow the space ship.

Follow @awolnation

Nottingham, UK

LITTLE MIX is the best British girl group since the Spice Girls. After winning The X Factor in 2011, they won over the hearts of millions. See them at home in the U.K. or even sooner in the U.S.

Follow @LittleMixOffic

June
5

Ibiza,Spain

DAVID GUETTA, French house music producer, party genius. It may seem like he's always in Ibiza, and that's because he is. Don't miss an encore of his legendary F*** Me I'm Famous party, featuring yachts, crowd surfing, and bikinis. Join his entourage on his international tour.

Follow @davidguetta
