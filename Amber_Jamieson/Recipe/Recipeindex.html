Chocolate Peanut Butter Cup Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter softened
  • 3/4 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 1/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup peanut butter chips
  • 10 chocolate covered peanut butter cups, cut into eighths
Calories: 230 | Total Fat: 13g | Cholesterol: 26mg

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  3. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, peanut butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  5. Combine the flour, cocoa, and baking soda; stir into the peanut butter mixture.
  6. Mix in the chocolate chips, peanut butter chips, and peanut butter cups.
  7. Drop by tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  8. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven.
  9. Let cool for 1 or 2 minutes on sheet before removing, or they will fall apart.
