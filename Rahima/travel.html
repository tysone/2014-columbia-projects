6 Places to Vist in Cape Town

Cape Town is a beautiful coastal city in South Africa and the second-most populous city in the country after Johannesburg. The city is famous for its harbour and well-known landmarks such as Table Mountain and Cape Point. Below are 6 places to go in Cape Town.

Aquila Safari

Aquila Safari is a big 5 Game Reserve close to Cape Town. Situated under two hours drive from the city, it is the ultimate African safari getaway. Aquila Game Reserve offers traditional game drives, safaris on horseback, quad biking, 4 x 4 mountain trails, golf and cherry picking in the valley.

Table Mountain

Table Mountain one of Cape Towns most recognized landmarks, is a flat-topped mountain that overlooks the city. Layers of sandstone that have been eroded by wind and water give the mountain its table shape. It is a popular tourist attraction that offers hiking, camping, and many other activities.

The Beach

Cape Town has one of the most spectacular and pristine beaches in the world, wrapping itself around the coast. Whatever you are looking for, from secluded bays perfect for water sports or sandy stretches perfect for sunbathing. You can find it here.

The Castle Military Museum

The Castle Military Museum is one of South Africas national monuments. It is the oldest building in South Africa and a tribute to the political and military history of the country and the Cape. Be sure to check out their impressive sword collection.

The West Coast National Park

An hour and a half drive from Cape Towns city centre is the West Coast national park. Its blue waters and pristine beaches are beautiful to behold. The coast is also a sanctuary for thousands of seabirds and various wildlife. This idyllic nature reserve is not to be missed.
